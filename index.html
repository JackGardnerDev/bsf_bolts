Who Are We?

For more than 45 years we have been supplying fasteners and related tooling for the repair or restoration of Vintage and Classic Cars, Vintage Motorcycles, Vintage Aircraft, Old and Rare Machinery and Model Engineering Projects.

We supply fasteners BSF (British Standard Fine), BSB (British Standard Brass), BSW (British Standard Whitworth), UNF (Unified National Fine) and UNC (Unified National Coarse) thread types.

We also supply tooling including taps and dies (individually or in sets) for those thread types, including a tap and die hire service.

Our Stock

We now have more then two thousand different items in our stock.

If you would like a copy of our catalogue, please contact us and we can email it to you or visit the catalogue page.

BSF: BSF bolts, setscrews, screws, nuts, locknuts, with head types including hex, cheese head, countersunk, roundhead, mushroom head, with slotted, Phillips and Allen drives, in a variety of materials, tensile strengths, and plating types, with diameters ranging from 3/16” to 1 ¼”, and lengths ranging from ¼” to 6”, as well as various types of Allthread. We also have a range of engineering taps and dies, including some rare left hand thread.

BA: Bolts and screws in a range of head types, and nuts, from size 0BA to size 12BA, as well as engineering taps and dies to size 12BA.

BSW: We stock a range of Whitworth fasteners in diameters from 1/8" to 1 1/4".

Other Items: We also have some items in brass thread (BSB - 26 TPI), cycle thread (BSCY - 20 and 26 TPI), UNF, UNC, as well as a range of flat and spring washers, and some miscellaneous hardware such as clevis pins, split pins and grease nipples. Many of our items are from military or aircraft sources, and some have a NSN code (NATO Stock Number) for exact identification.

We also now stock a large range of slotted wood screws in brass and steel, in various gauges and lengths - please enquire for details.
